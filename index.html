Fill in the chart below to test your knowledge of the PMBOK® 6 Process Groups & Knowledge Areas. This is useful for studying for the PMP® certification exam.

Type an answer into the gray box and hit return. If it's not an answer, it will turn pink and you can try again. If it's a process in the wrong spot, it will turn gold. Note that individual answers must be given exactly as shown on the original chart from the guide, including spelling and spacing, but for processes (and only processes) capitalization doesn't matter.
